Liverpool F.C

is a professional football club in Liverpool, England, that competes in the Premier League, the top tier of English football. Domestically, the club has won nineteen League titles, seven FA Cups, a record eight League Cups and fifteen FA Community Shields. In continental and international competitions, the club has won six European Cups, more than any other English club, three UEFA Cups, four UEFA Super Cups and one FIFA Club World Cup.

The trophies

Liverpool football club is a club with a lot of history between their glory days in the '70s and '80s and their recent renaissance under their new manager it is no wonder that they have won everything there is.

They have won 65 trophies in total which is the most in the whole of England. Ahead of their longtime rivals Manchester United. This year has been a historical one because they won their first FIFA clubs world cup and premier league.
